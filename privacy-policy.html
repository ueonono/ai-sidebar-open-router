AI Sidebar — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

In short: AI Sidebar is 100% BYOK (“bring your own key”). It has no servers of its own, performs no analytics or tracking, and collects, stores and sells nothing. Everything stays on your device, except the requests you explicitly make, which go directly from your browser to the AI provider you chose, using your key.

1. Who operates this extension

AI Sidebar is an open-source browser extension developed by Florian Martins. The full source code is available at github.com/FlorianMartins/firefox-ai-sidebar.

2. Data the extension handles (all stored locally)

The following is kept only in your browser via storage.local. It is never synced to any account or sent to us — we have no server to send it to:

  • API keys / credentials you enter (or obtain via OpenRouter sign-in).
  • Your settings (chosen provider/model, language, layout, toggles, etc.).
  • Conversation history, if you enable saving it (off-by-default options exist; you can clear it any time).

3. Page, tab and element content

When you ask for it — by enabling the “Page” context, picking tabs, using the element picker, the translate/summarize actions, or the webmail “Reply with AI” button — the extension reads text (and, for the element picker, a screenshot of the element you click) from the current page so the AI can act on it. This content is included in the request to your chosen AI provider only for that request. It is not stored by us and not sent anywhere else. The extension does not read or monitor pages in the background for any other purpose.

4. Where your data goes

The only network requests the extension makes are to the AI provider endpoint you configured (e.g. Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Gemini, Mistral, Groq, DeepSeek, xAI, Perplexity, OpenRouter, or a local server such as Ollama / LM Studio), authenticated with your key. Those providers are independent third parties and process your prompts under their own privacy policies and terms. Please review the policy of whichever provider(s) you connect. We are not a party to that processing and never receive a copy.

5. What we do NOT do

  • No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking pixels, no advertising IDs.
  • No remote/operator servers; no remote code execution — all code ships inside the extension.
  • No collection, sale, rental or sharing of your data with anyone.
  • No background reading of your browsing.

6. Permissions and why they are used

  • storage — save your keys, settings and (optional) history locally.
  • tabs, activeTab, scripting, host access (<all_urls>) — read the page/tab/element you point at, and let the optional Agent mode act on the page you ask it to.
  • identity — optional one-click OpenRouter sign-in (OAuth).
  • contextMenus — right-click actions (translate, summarize, improve…).
  • clipboardWrite — copy AI output when you ask.
  • sidePanel (Chromium) / sidebar_action (Firefox) — display the sidebar.

7. Microphone / camera

The extension does not access your microphone or camera.

8. Children

AI Sidebar is a general-purpose developer/productivity tool and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change and the new version will be published at this URL and in the repository.
